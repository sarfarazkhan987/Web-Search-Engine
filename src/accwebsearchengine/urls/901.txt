The workshops are delivered by experienced lawyers and professionals in the intellectual property space. The workshops are designed to provide essential information on the importance of intellectual property, such as patents, trademarks, copyright etc. to startups and students/faculty members who may have a commercializable idea or research. The workshop series is led by Professor Myra Tawfik, Professor of Law and EPICentre Professor of IP Commercialization & Strategy. Check out our Event Page for upcoming workshops. Registration is free!
