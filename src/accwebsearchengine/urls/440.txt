Campus tours are offered Monday to Friday at 10:30 am and 2:30 pm and are led by a current UWin Student Ambassador. We are ready to address your questions about student life and academics and show you around our beautiful campus. The tour will include a general overview of the main campus and residences, as well as an opportunity to visit our downtown sites for the School of Creative Arts and the School of Social Work. Please allow approximately 1.5 hours for your visit of main campus and please indicate when booking if you would like to visit the downtown campus. Please wear comfortable footwear, and dress for the weather as we will proceed, rain or shine. To book your campus visit, please telephone 1-800-864-2860 (toll free) or 519-973-7014 (local). Explore UWindsor's Campus Visit UWindsor in person or online Take a virtual tour of UWindsor UWindsor is one of the most welcoming, international schools in Canada. We're a comprehensive university where award-winning faculty, researchers and students come together to grow and solve the problems of the world. Whether you’re looking to fulfil your dream, hone your craft or build a career, there’s a place for you here in Windsor. UWindsor is creating a world-class hub for innovation, research and the latest in classroom teaching technology. With 260 programs, including more than 70 at the graduate and professional levels, our students experience life-changing learning and are involved in world-changing research. The University of Windsor sits on the traditional territory of the Three Fires Confederacy of First Nations, comprised of the Ojibwa, the Odawa, and the Potawatomi.
