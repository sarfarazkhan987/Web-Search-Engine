We are your family physician's office while on campus. We provide confidential, student-centred health care, including comprehensive medical care, counselling, and referrals. Patients must be registered University of Windsor students. IMPORTANT NOTICE – STUDENT HEALTH SERVICES Effective Immediately All initial and recheck counselling appointments will now require 24 hours cancellation notice to avoid a charge. Effective November 1, 2018 Short Medical Notes and Medical Certificates will be provided only when the student is seen by a physician at SHS within 72 hours of the medical issue or illness. If you require a medical note or certificate and you cannot see a SHS physician within 72 hours of your medical issue, please see a physician at one of the clinics nearby. See our website for information regarding other clinics. There will be no exceptions made for this new policy. Sincerely, Dr. M.
