Typically, the deadlines to apply to graduate are: March 1st for the Spring Convocation August 1st for the Fall Convocation held in October Typically, late applications to attend the ceremony, and the associated non-refundable fee, will be accepted from March 2nd to one month prior to the Spring ceremony at $80 August 2nd to one month prior to the Fall ceremony at $80 To apply to graduate, please log on to UWinsite Student. No applications will be accepted within the four weeks prior to the start of Convocation.
