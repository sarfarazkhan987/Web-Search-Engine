To help better prepare our start-ups in their entrepreneurial journey, EPICentre is pleased to announce the launch of its free EPIC Legal Triage service for its members. The service will be run by Professor Myra Tawfik, our EPICentre Professor of IP Commercialization and Strategy, and her law students and will provide preliminary business and intellectual property legal information and legal coaching to EPICentre’s members. This program will be piloted in late September 2019.
