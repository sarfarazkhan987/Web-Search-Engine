Enhance your personal and professional life by learning Mandarin! This course serves as an introduction to Mandarin Chinese, and will focus on: proper pronunciation, listening comprehension, oral communication skills, essential grammatical structures, and introduction to the written language. Whether your goal is to open up employment opportunities, or open up your world to a potential billion-plus Mandarin-speaking friends, this course is an excellent place to start. When: Every Tuesday and Thursday, 5:00pm-7:00pm, March 3 to April 30, 2020.
