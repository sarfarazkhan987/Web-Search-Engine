and Format-checking deadline: last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet Phase I deadline for Fall term 2019. September 27: Fall convocation deadline: last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers in the Office of Graduate Studies for Fall Convocation 2019; and Phase I deadline: last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase I (100% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for the Fall term 2019. October 2: Financial Drop Date for Fall semester October 14: Thanksgiving Day (statutory holiday) (no classes, University closed). October 12-20 Reading Week October 15: Format checking deadline for Phase II: last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet the Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Fall term 2019. October 18: Phase II deadline: last day for completion of all requirements for graduate degrees under Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Fall term 2019. October 19: Fall Convocation. November 13: Last day to voluntarily withdraw from Fall term courses. November 28: Last day to send a doctoral dissertation to the External Examiner for a January 2020 defense. December 15: Winter term fees are due and payable. Deadlines for depositing theses, dissertations and major papers before the end of Fall term 2019: Friday, December 13 at 4:30 p.m.: format checking deadline for Fall term 2019: last day to submit for format review dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office. Friday, December 20 at 4:30 p.m.: deposit deadline for Fall term 2019: last day to complete the final submission (deposit) of dissertations, theses and major papers in the Graduate Studies office for the Fall term 2019. Students who do not complete the final deposit by December 20th must register for the Winter term 2020 - refer to details about registration and tuition refund deadlines for Winter term 2020. Dec. 24, 2019- Jan. 1, 2020 University offices closed for Christmas recess. Winter term 2020 January 6, 2020 Winter term classes begin January 14, 2020 The earliest date a PhD defense can be scheduled in 2020. January 17, 2020 Academic Add/Drop Date for Winter term: Last day for late registration and change of courses January 20, 2020 Last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office for students who are planning to meet the Phase I deadline (100% tuition) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020. January 27, 2020 Phase I deadline: Last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase I (100% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020. February 2, 2020 Financial Drop date for Winter semester February 10, 2020 Last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office for students who are planning to meet the Phase II deadline (50% tuition) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020 February 14, 2020 Phase II deadline: Last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020 February 15-23, 2020 Reading Week February 17, 2020 Family Day Holiday: University closed February 21, 2020 University offices closed March 1, 2020 Last day to file an application for Spring graduation March 13 Last day to voluntarily withdraw from Winter term courses.
