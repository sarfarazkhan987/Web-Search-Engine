STEPS sessions are held throughout the fall and winter semester to introduce practical methods that can help you improve your learning and study skills across disciplines. STEPS workshops are free of charge and no registration is required to attend our workshops. If you would like more information about the STEPS program, please call extension 1414 or email success@uwindsor.ca.
