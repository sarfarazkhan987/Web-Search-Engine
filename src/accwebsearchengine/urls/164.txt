Our world-class researchers collaborate with industry and community organizations in bringing new ideas and technologies into our region providing industry the competitive edge in the local, national and global markets. We are committed to fostering and continuously improving our relationships with industry and helping them remain vibrant and sustainable through the mobility of people and ideas.
