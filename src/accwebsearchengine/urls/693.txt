Peer tutors provide support with first and second-year Engineering course material. Mentors Third and fourth-year students who excelled in first and second-year courses are encouraged to apply. Tutors will receive a $1,000 award. WINONE will select approximately ten students to mentor and tutor junior students during weekly tutorials and exam prep sessions. Mentees Registration is not required. Please see below for a list of drop-in hours.
