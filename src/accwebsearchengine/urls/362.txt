Applicants with degrees in related fields will be considered but will normally require strengthening of their background in Industrial and Manufacturing Systems Engineering. Our objective is to attract the best students and to educate them in the rigors of scientific research. We provide a scholarly and dynamic environment with attention to scientific discipline, detail and accuracy.
