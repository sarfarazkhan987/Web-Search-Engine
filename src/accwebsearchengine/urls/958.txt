Audition requirements by instrument Mastery of Core Music Theory Concepts While UWindsor does not require incoming music students pass a Music Theory Test, incoming and prospective students are highly encouraged to download and complete our online Music Theory Rudiments Diagnostic Test. Completion of these worksheets should take no longer than a half hour. Once completed, download and check your work against the test key. Audition days for future students will have an opportunity to warm up and rehearse with your accompanist. You will have a set time for your audition. The overall schedule for an audition day is typically as follows: 12:00pm: Audition and rehearsals begin 1:00pm: Optional SoCA program inforamtion session 2:00pm Optional buidling(s) tour Please feel free to contact the School of Creative Arts at soca@uwindsor.ca or extension 2829 if you have any questions.
