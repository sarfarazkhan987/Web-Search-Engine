The various teams within the Student Experience portfolio and their Directors, Managers and Team Leads report to Ryan Flannagan the Associate Vice-President, Student Experience. In total, there is over 70 professional staff and dozens of student leaders who are working to help UWindsor students achieve academic and personal success. In the event you have questions about any of these services listed below, or are experiencing challenges, or you think a member of the larger Student Experience team has done something positive in support of your experience, let us know by writing to studentexperience@uwindsor.ca and someone from the team will get back to you promptly. As a team, we are dedicated to trying to make the student experience here at the University of Windsor the best that we can and when we hear from you (good or bad) that helps us.
