As a mid-size university supporting 16,000 students, UWindsor provides a broad range of academic opportunities. As an undergraduate or graduate student, you can choose from over 120 majors and minors and more than 70 masters and doctoral degrees in subjects like Engineering, Forensics and Business. At the heart of all of our programs is our exceptional faculty, men and women who are leading scholars in their fields and dedicated mentors in the classroom.
