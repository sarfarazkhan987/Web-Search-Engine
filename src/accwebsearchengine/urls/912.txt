Membership is free for students and recent graduates from University of Windsor and St. Clair College. Check out the Make It & Take It workshop series held in the makerspace here. Location: Room 1133, Ed Lumley Centre for Engineering Innovation (CEI) The hours of operation for the makerspace vary each semester. Check back here or follow us on Facebook page for updates regarding the space. To apply for the membership, complete the application and drop off at the makerspace or email to epicentre@uwindsor.ca. You can also pick up the application package at the makerspace. Click here to read the member handbook. Contact epicentre@uwindsor.ca for further information.
